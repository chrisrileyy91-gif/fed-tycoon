Privacy Policy

CHAIR: The Fed Game

The short version

CHAIR collects no data. None. No analytics, no tracking, no accounts, no network requests. The game runs entirely on your device and never communicates with any server.

What the game stores

CHAIR saves your progress — deck unlocks and best scores — to your device's local storage. This data never leaves your device. It is not transmitted, synced, backed up to any server, or accessible to anyone other than you. Deleting the app removes it.

Network access

CHAIR makes zero network requests. The game contains no ads, no analytics SDKs, no crash reporters, no social features, and no server-side components of any kind. It works fully offline.

Third-party services

None. CHAIR does not integrate with any third-party services, advertising networks, or analytics platforms.

Children's privacy

CHAIR does not collect personal information from anyone, including children. The game is rated 4+ and contains no objectionable content, in-app purchases, or external links within gameplay.

Changes to this policy

If CHAIR ever adds features that involve data collection (such as leaderboards or cloud sync), this policy will be updated before those features ship. The effective date below will reflect any changes.
